Horses and Facts

Horses are beautiful cratures and riding them fascinates a lot. They are with the humans from around 4000BC.

  • Scientific Name: Equus ferus caballus
  • Average Height: 160 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 28 years
  • Habitat: Stables-open spaces & grass

Horses play a vital role within human cultures. They live in group of dozens in the wild. They are now trained to be ridden or driven in a variety of sporting competitions. Patroling Mountainous borders can not be done better without horses.
